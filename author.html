Get to know him

Early Childhood

Rajarshi Datta Roy commonly known as R.D.Roy in the art world, is the author of You Can Draw! art book series for children. He is a Kolkata based visual artist and art teacher. He completed his schooling from Salt Lake School (English Medium), Kolkata and graduated from University of Calcutta. Ever since his childhood days his one and only love has been pursuing Art. he learnt the basics of Art from his father, who was also an accomplished artist, Mr.Parimal Datta Roy and other renowned art mentors of Kolkata. As a child R.D.Roy had won the President of India's award for Best Child Artist in 1983 and All India Camel Colour Contest for school children in 1985.

Achievements

R.D.Roy is now an accomplished Painter proficient in painting figuratives and landscapes using Watercolour on paper, Charcoal and Oil Pastel on Paper and Acrylic on Canvas.

He was invited to a two months long International Watercolour Artists Residency, Art Camp and Exhibition by Vermont Studio Centre at Vermont, USA and Frankfurt, Germany in April-May 2014. He was honoured with the All India award for best Watercolour Painting by the Camel Art Foundation in January 2015, Prafulla Dahanukar Art Foundation, Mumbai, in May 2015. His paintings are in private and corporate collections both in India and abroad.

He is also a successful 2D Animation Artist and has worked as an Animator in the mega series 'Mr.Bean' which is now on air in Pogo Television channel. This was done for Varga Studios, Budapest, Hungary.

R.D.Roy is a Visiting Lecturer at the Animation department of Satyajit Ray Film and Television Institute (SRFTI), Kolkata, Govt. of India.

His solo endeavour- You Can Draw! Art Book Series

With his 20 years of experience in the field of Art, Mr. R.D. Roy came out with an unique series of books on learning art for younger generation titled "You can Draw!" Volume 1 to 6 and published under his own brand Chitrali Publications.

Important features of You Can Draw! series of six art books:

  1. Step by step methodical approach to learn art.
  2. Learning to draw still lifes, landscapes, animals, birds, humans using simple geometric shapes.
  3. Special emphasis has been laid on the techniques and application of colour and paints.
  4. The photos of the steps of painting a picture with easy to understand instructions are featured.
  5. Paper craft, clay modelling, paper collage, stencil printing art has been introduced to open other avenues of creative expression for a child.
  6. 100gsm ITC SSP good quality cartridge paper has been used to support application of oil pastels as well as watercolours.

His Mission and Vision

  • These books will definitely help the child to draw well and easily from memory and be even more creative.
  • The series aims to help children to develop their interest in art and lays a solid foundation for their creative pursuits in future.
  • This art course will help children to concentrate better in their academics.
  • The series of books is crafted in such a manner that it may be considered as an effective alternative to offline classes.
