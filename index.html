YOU CAN DRAW!

Key features of our books

Step by step approach

Learning art made easy with step by step approach with easy to follow instructions.

Foundational course

It is a foundational course on art for children which lays a solid base for their creative pursuits in future.

Improves concentration

Beneficial for children with concentration issues. Learning art helps children with attention deficit or hyperactivity disorders to calm down and be focussed.

Skills taught

Pencil shading, drawing and colouring human figures, animals, objects and landscapes, paper-craft, paper-collage and clay-modelling

You Can Draw- Volume 1

For age group: 4+ to 6+ yrs
Price: Rs 150

A colouring book of 20 pages in which kids have to colour already drawn cartoon humans, animals, etc with colour pencils, wax crayons or oil pastels.

You Can Draw- Volume 2

For age group: 5+ to 7+ yrs
Price: Rs 150

Humans, animals, landscapes are drawn with dotted lines. Kids have to draw with pencil along these dotted lines and colour with wax crayons, oil pastels or colour pencils.
